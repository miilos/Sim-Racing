Rules

Malfunctions

During a race, every vehicle has a chance of malfunctioning. In that case, takes a small time-out from the race. The chances of a malfunction are:

  • Sports cars: 12%
  • Terrain cars: 3%
  • Sports bikes: 18%
  • Cross bikes: 3%
  • Trucks: 6%

Types

The main thing to remember here is the types of vehicles you'll find:

  • Cars (sports and terrain cars)
  • Bikes (sports and cross bikes)
  • Trucks

The race

Once you select all vehicles that'll participate, you can start a race. Once a race is finished, you can check the standings for all vehicles, or a certain type of vehicle.
